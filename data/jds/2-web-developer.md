## About the Opportunity
Join a leading Engineering & Construction firm dedicated to integrating cutting-edge technology into the built environment. This role offers a unique opportunity for an aspiring developer to drive digital transformation within a stable, growing industry. You will have the chance to build high-impact tools from the ground up while accelerating your professional growth through mentorship and diverse project exposure.

## Role & Responsibilities
The Web Developer will be a key member of our technical team, reporting directly to the Head of the Department. Your primary purpose is to design, develop, and maintain scalable web applications that enhance our operational efficiency and client experience. You will bridge the gap between complex engineering requirements and intuitive digital interfaces, ensuring our systems are production-ready, secure, and future-proof.

## Key Responsibilities
**1. Frontend Development & User Experience**
* Design and implement responsive, scalable websites that function seamlessly across all device types.
* Enhance user interfaces by creating intuitive layouts and engaging animations to improve overall UX.
* Translate complex technical requirements into clean, maintainable frontend code.
* Conduct usability testing to identify and resolve interface bottlenecks.

**2. Backend Engineering & System Architecture**
* Develop robust, production-grade server-side logic using Node.js, Express, Python, and FastAPI.
* Build and maintain scalable APIs to ensure efficient communication between the frontend and database.
* Implement rigorous web security protocols to protect sensitive company and project data.
* Optimize system performance to handle increasing loads as our digital footprint expands.

**3. DevOps & Cloud Infrastructure**
* Manage the deployment pipeline using Docker and Kubernetes for consistent environment orchestration.
* Configure and maintain Linux-based environments to ensure high availability of services.
* Leverage cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP) to deploy and scale production systems.
* Collaborate on CI/CD workflows to automate testing and deployment processes.

**4. AI Integration & Innovation**
* Explore and integrate Artificial Intelligence capabilities into web applications to drive automation.
* Implement modern AI concepts such as Large Language Models (LLMs), Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and Vector Databases.
* Stay current with emerging web technologies to propose innovative solutions for engineering challenges.
* Prototype new features that leverage AI to improve data retrieval and user interaction.
