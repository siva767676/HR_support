## Role & Responsibilities
The Site Engineer (Structural) is responsible for overseeing the execution of all reinforced concrete work across multiple project towers. Working closely with the Project Manager, this role ensures that structural elements are constructed according to design specifications, safety standards, and quality benchmarks to ensure long-term stability and compliance.

## Key Responsibilities
**1. Structural Execution & Supervision**
* Oversee day-to-day structural activities across multiple towers to ensure adherence to project timelines.
* Supervise the placement of reinforcement steel and formwork for RCC elements.
* Monitor concrete pouring operations to prevent defects and ensure structural integrity.
* Manage on-site labor and subcontractors to maintain high productivity and safety standards.
* Interpret structural drawings and translate them into precise site execution.

**2. Quality Assurance & Control (QA/QC)**
* Conduct rigorous quality checks at every stage of the construction process.
* Implement QA/QC protocols to ensure all materials used meet project specifications.
* Perform slump tests, cube casting, and other necessary field tests for concrete quality.
* Identify structural deviations early and implement corrective actions immediately.
* Maintain detailed quality logs and inspection reports for audit purposes.

**3. Technical Coordination & Planning**
* Coordinate with structural consultants to resolve design discrepancies or site constraints.
* Utilize AutoCAD to review drawings and propose necessary on-site modifications.
* Collaborate with the Project Manager to align structural progress with the overall project schedule.
* Facilitate communication between the design team and the execution team to minimize errors.
* Prepare daily and weekly progress reports regarding structural milestones.

## Qualifications & Skills
**Education:** B.E. Civil
**Year of Experience:** 4 to 6 years
**Core Competencies:**
* Proficiency in RCC (Reinforced Cement Concrete) construction.
* Strong knowledge of QA/QC standards and testing procedures.
* Advanced skill in AutoCAD for drawing review and modification.
* Ability to manage multiple work fronts across high-rise towers.
* Effective communication and stakeholder management skills.
